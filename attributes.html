Tags in HTML are the basic building blocks of a webpage. They are written inside angle brackets (< >) and tell the browser how to display or structure content. Tags can be container tags (with opening and closing parts like <p></p>), non-container/self-closing tags like (<br> or <img>), and can also be nested (one tag inside another).
